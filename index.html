Selected Work

A few examples of systems I’ve built or led that delivered meaningful scale, reliability, and business value.

AI Support Assistant — Intuit

Built a Slack-based AI assistant using OpenAI, RAG, Slack APIs, Jira APIs, and indexed internal knowledge sources. The system supported more than 220 channels, handled roughly 85% of support message traffic, and helped reduce support volume by about 27%.

Sanctions Screening Platform Migration

Led architecture and implementation for the migration from Amber Road to LexisNexis, including backend services and cloud infrastructure. The resulting platform supported daily screenings, audit workflows, and batch processing at scale across 130M+ Credit Karma records and 14M+ Mailchimp records.

AI Performance Review Assistant — Zoox

Designed and optimized an AI-assisted review pipeline that generated more than 26,000 structured review artifacts for 2,800+ employees. Improved throughput with parallelism and batching, while reducing AI costs significantly through caching and resilience patterns.

Subscription & Catalog Modernization

Led modernization efforts from legacy integrations to Spring Boot microservices in AWS, improved maintainability and performance of high-traffic APIs, and introduced idempotent event-driven designs to reduce production issues in critical signup flows.
